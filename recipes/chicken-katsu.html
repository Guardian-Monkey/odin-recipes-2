Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken katsu is Japanese-style fried chicken. It also be used to make tonkatsu by using pork cutlets instead of chicken. Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • four skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • simply seasoned with salt and pepper
  • All-purpose flour; helps seal in the moisture, adds flavor, and promotes browning.
  • Panko; Panko bread crumbs are responsible for katsu's signature crunch.
  • An egg; adds moisture and gives the Panko something to stick to.
  • Oil; Opt for a neutral oil with a high smoke point, such as canola or vegetable oil.
